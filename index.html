Computer Aided Device for Detection of Heart Murmurs

TECHNICAL PROJECT | Electrical & Electronics

Device with input and output peripherals

Display with Funtionality

Breadboard Prototyping

Stethoscope input


Designed and developed a Computer Aided Device for the detection of Heart Murmurs. The device uses minimalistic interfaces and economic components. The input is a heart beat recording taken from the stethoscope. Using Support Vector Machines in MATLAB, the device can classify between normal heart beat and heart beats with murmur as a result of an infection. The device has four functions namely, record, playback, hear and delete. It was tested with positive results at Narayana Hrudayalaya, a leading cardio specialist hospital situated in Bangalore.

Road Oddity Alert Device

TECHNICAL PROJECT | Electrical & Electronics

The device placed in a car

The RF transmitter and display

Breadboard Prototyping

Breadboard Prototyping


The idea for ROAD was born out of the need for effective safety measures in regions where road signs could not be placed. The Road oddity alert device or “ROAD” is a prototype device, developed to act as an alert system installed in vehicles. It will alert the driver with visuals and sound when the vehicle approaches an oddity in the road. A small RF transmitter is placed near area that cannot have a road sign. As the vehicle approaches the danger zoe, the housed reciever will notify the driver. The devices uses RF-modules and PIC microcontrollers.
