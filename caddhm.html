Computer Aided Device for Detection of Heart Murmurs

TECHNICAL PROJECT | Electrical & Electronics

Device with input and output peripherals

Display with Funtionality

Breadboard Prototyping

Stethoscope input


Designed and developed a Computer Aided Device for the detection of Heart Murmurs. The device uses minimalistic interfaces and economic components. The input is a heart beat recording taken from the stethoscope. Using Support Vector Machines in MATLAB, the device can classify between normal heart beat and heart beats with murmur as a result of an infection. The device has four functions namely, record, playback, hear and delete. It was tested with positive results at Narayana Hrudayalaya, a leading cardio-specialist hsopital situated in Bangalore.
